Internship

Provide exposure to computer and modern technology literacy. Use technology to build a culture of love for education and unity for Africa. Improve communication and presentation skills. Teach African Students the discipline of Agile Software Development And Delivery which stimulates creativity and innovation making young Africans producers instead of consumers of technology. Provide students with unique skills that bridge the gap between formal education and first job requirements.

Take A Course

Entrepreneurship & Leadership Development Seminars

Inspire African students to share and value their business ideas in order to build confidence to take on a journey of entrepreneurship. Equip young Africans with quality leadership skills so that they understand what is expected to become the leaders of tomorrow. Use technology as a tool to trigger creativity in the world of entrepreurship. Build a network of young African leaders to come and work together for a common purpose to find solutions to African problems. Create a platform that enables young talented Africans to use technology and bring about positive change in Africa.

Take A Course

Training Workshops

Provide equal opportunities to all African students from diverse educational backgrounds to embark on the journey of learning free and open source technology. Create a safe environment for African students from diverse cultural, religious and ethnical backgrounds. Provide African students with proffessional career guidance and exposure to modern technology. Expose the larger majority of African students to the culture of bring, share and learn. Reach out to people from underpriviledged backgrounds to introduce them to basic technology skills.

Take A Course

Roundtable Discussions

Raise awareness on social and economic justice issues that the world is facing in general and that Africa continues to struggle with in particular. Help African students to learn more about the Indian culture in order to better integrate themselves into the Indian Society. Develop the culture of academic excellence recognition among the African students community by awarding the top achievers. Provide a platform to showcase and demonstrate the beauty of diversity of African culture through Cultural Exchange Events. Build an environment for respect and unity among young African and other students to agree on openly debating on differences in cultures but more importantly on the common challenges that their respective societies face.
